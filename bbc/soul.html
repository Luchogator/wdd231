What Inspires Me

Nature's Patterns

The mathematical precision of natural forms—from the Fibonacci sequence in flower petals to the fractal patterns of coastlines—reminds me that beauty and logic are intertwined.

Musical Rhythms

The way music can evoke emotions without words fascinates me. I draw parallels between musical composition and code structure—both require rhythm, pattern recognition, and creative problem-solving.

Family Heritage

Preserving and restoring family memories connects me to my roots. These restoration projects have taught me patience and attention to detail that translates directly to my coding practices.

Featured Restoration Project

1958 Philips Radio Restoration

This vintage Philips radio belonged to my grandfather. After sitting in storage for decades, I carefully restored both its exterior and internal components.

The process involved:

  • Cleaning and refinishing the wooden cabinet
  • Replacing capacitors and resistors
  • Rewiring the power supply for safety
  • Documenting the entire process

The radio now works perfectly and serves as both a functional device and a piece of family history on display in my workspace.
