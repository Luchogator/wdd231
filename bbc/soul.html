My Grandmother's Philips Radio

This vintage Philips radio belonged to my grandmother. After years of sitting silent in a dusty corner, I undertook the project of restoring it to working condition while preserving its original aesthetic.

The Restoration Process

  • Carefully disassembled the radio to assess internal components
  • Replaced damaged capacitors while maintaining period-appropriate parts
  • Restored the wooden cabinet with traditional techniques
  • Repaired the dial mechanism and tuning system
  • Added a subtle modern audio input while preserving the original radio functionality

This project taught me that technology can be both preserved and adapted—a philosophy I bring to my development work. Sometimes the most elegant solutions honor the past while embracing the future.
